Regular Check-Up

We prioritise the health of our community and provide free health checks twice a day, morning and evening at our Dharmasala. Our devoted healthcare staff guarantees that everyone, regardless of financial position, receives the appropriate attention. We emphasise preventative care, early identification, and timely treatment through daily check-ups, resulting in a happier and more healthier community.
