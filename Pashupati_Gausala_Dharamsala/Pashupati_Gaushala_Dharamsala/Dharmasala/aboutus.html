About Us

Our Mission

Provide free and accessible healthcare to pilgrims, integrating modern medicine and traditional healing with empathy, respect, and support for holistic well-being on their spiritual journey.

History

In the wake of the 1990 earthquake, the Marwadi group provided compassionate aid to the affected people in Tundikhel. Prime Minister Juddha Shumsher recognized their selfless efforts and encouraged them to continue helping the community. Shree Pashupati Gaushala Dharamshala was founded in 1997, demonstrating their unwavering commitment to serving those in need.

Our Services

Our organization is committed to ensuring a comfortable and enriching pilgrimage experience for all. We provide a variety of accommodations, including rooms with attached bathrooms, spacious halls, and kitchen serving delectable vegetarian meals. With a strong focus on well-being, we offer a comprehensive range of health services, including free ambulance service, homeopathic and allopathic checkups, regular hemodialysis and so on. For those in need, we extend support by providing free artificial legs every alternate Saturday, overseen by our dedicated doctor-coordinator. Additionally, we offer nourishing services like free meals every Saturday, pure cow milk, and pure vegetarian meals to enhance their spiritual journey.
