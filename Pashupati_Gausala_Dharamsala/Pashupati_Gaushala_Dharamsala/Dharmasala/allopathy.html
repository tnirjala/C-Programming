Allopathy

For immediate medical care and modern treatment options, Shree Pashupati Gaushala Dharamashala offers Allopathy treatment daily from 7 am to 8 am. Our skilled medical professionals utilize evidence-based medicine and state-of-the-art techniques to address health conditions and provide necessary medical attention. Allopathy is known for its scientific approach to diagnosis and treatment, and we are dedicated to providing these services free of cost to our visitors and pilgrims. The well-being and health of our guests are of utmost importance, and our Allopathy treatment ensures they receive the care they need during their stay with us.
