About Us

Founded in 1997 (1939 A.D.) by the esteemed Marwari Community of Nepal, Shree Pashupati Gaushala Dharamshala has remained persistent in its commitment to Gau Sewa (cow service) and humanitarian initiatives. We offer affordable or even free lodging for those seeking temporary accommodation during their spiritual visits to nearby temples.

Activities

Rooms

The rooms are affordable, making it a great option for budget travelers.

Fooding

We serves delicious and affordable vegetarian dishes,at reasonable price.

Cow Protection

Sheltering, caring, preserving cows, daily check-ups and rescues.

Health Care

We provide free healthcare services in Dharamsala, including artificial limb plantation, hemodialysis, health check-ups, homeopathy and allopathy treatments, ambulance services, and funeral vehicle arrangements. Our commitment to exceptional healthcare is driven by our belief that service is the people's religion.
