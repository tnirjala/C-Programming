Fooding

Our canteen at Shree Pashupati Gaushala Dharmashala is a friendly area that promotes unity and sustenance. It can seat a big number of people and provides the ideal atmosphere for visitors to enjoy delectable vegetarian food. Our broad cuisine appeals to a wide range of tastes and preferences, from curries to delicious rice and chapatis. We provide delectable meals for as little as 120 rupees, assuring affordability without sacrificing quality. Everyone may enjoy the pleasures of delicious food and the atmosphere of unity at our canteen.


We recognise that everyone has different tastes, which is why we provide self-cooking facilities. This option allows visitors to make their own meals, giving them the freedom and flexibility to produce recipes that suit their tastes and dietary needs.


We hope to develop a sense of community and compassion within our Dharmasala through our Saturday free food programme. It's our way of giving back and making sure no one goes hungry. Our hardworking employees and volunteers prepare and serve these unique meals with love and care.
