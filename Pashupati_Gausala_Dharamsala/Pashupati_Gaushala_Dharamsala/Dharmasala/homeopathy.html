About Us

At Shree Pashupati Gaushala Dharamashala, we embrace the holistic approach of Homeopathy treatment, providing our visitors and pilgrims with natural and effective remedies for their health concerns. Our experienced Homeopathy practitioners conduct daily treatment sessions from 2 pm to 4 pm. With a focus on individualized care and gentle healing, Homeopathy offers a safe and non-intrusive method to address various ailments. We take pride in offering these Homeopathy sessions free of cost, ensuring that everyone has access to this alternative and beneficial form of healthcare.
