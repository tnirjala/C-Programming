Accommodation

Our Dharmasala offers a variety of lodging alternatives, with over 200 rooms tailored to meet a variety of requirements and budgets. We take pleasure in providing reasonable and comfortable accommodations, making it a great alternative for travellers looking for a tranquil and pleasant stay.


Our hotel prices are quite affordable, ranging from Rs.400 to Rs.800, guaranteeing that anybody may enjoy the peace and warmth of our Dharmasala without breaking the bank. Each room is outfitted with modern facilities such as a cool fan and a handy connected bathroom, guaranteeing a comfortable and convenient stay.


Our rooms variety assures that we have something to satisfy everyone's tastes. We have everything you need, whether it's a cosy single room, a more sumptuous deluxe accommodation, or a vast hall for larger parties.


If you decide to rent a room at our Dharmasala, all you need to do is bring a valid ID card with you for verification.Online booking is not currently accessible.
