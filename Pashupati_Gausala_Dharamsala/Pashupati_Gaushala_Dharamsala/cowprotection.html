Cow Protection

Cows hold a profound significance in our lives, and they have always been cherished and revered in our community. The very name of our Dharmasala derives from our long-standing tradition of providing shelter to these sacred animals. With immense pride, we currently house and care for a herd of 200 cows. A team of two competent veterinarians and three committed technicians guarantees that our cows get the finest care possible. Their frequent health checks are proof of our persistent commitment to their well-being.


We prioritise nutrition, and our cows are well-fed. We feed them a balanced diet that includes mineral-rich cereals , corn, mineral powder,healthy green grass and many more.


Our mission goes beyond sheltering; we also play a crucial role in rescuing cows that find themselves abandoned or struggling in the streets. It is our profound responsibility to provide them with a safe haven, where they can live free from harm and fear.


In addition to our care for cows, we are delighted to offer pure cow milk at just Rs. 130. Our commitment to the well-being of these magnificent animals extends to providing fresh and wholesome milk, ensuring that our community enjoys the goodness of nature at an affordable price. With every sip of our pure cow milk, you not only experience its richness but also support our noble cause of caring for these beloved creatures.
