Rules & Regulations

Rules and regulations for Dharamshala (Pilgrim Shelter):

1.Specific check-in and check-out timings is enforced to manage the accommodation facilities efficiently.

2.Smoking, consumption of alcohol, or any form of substance abuse may be prohibited within the dharamshala premises.

3.Pilgrims are expected to maintain a peaceful and respectful environment within the dharamshala, avoiding loud noises or disturbances.

4. Proper utilization of shared facilities like bathrooms and common areas should be observed.

5.Pilgrims may be required to comply with any additional rules and regulations set by the dharamshala management.

Rules and regulations for Gaushala (Cow Shelter):

1 The primary rule in a gaushala is to ensure the safety and well-being of the cows. No harm or mistreatment of the cows is allowed.

2. No unauthorized entry: Only authorized personnel, volunteers, or visitors with permission may enter the gaushala premises.

3. No feeding without permission: Visitors are generally not allowed to feed the cows without permission from the gaushala authorities.

4. Visitors are expected to behave respectfully towards the cows, the staff, and other visitors.
