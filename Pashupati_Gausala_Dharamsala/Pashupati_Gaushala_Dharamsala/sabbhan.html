Ambulance & Sabbhan

At Shree Pashupati Gaushala Dharmashala, your safety and well-being are our top priorities. We provide round-the-clock ambulance services for quick and efficient medical assistance, ensuring the health of all our visitors and pilgrims. Our trained staff and medical professionals are available to assist with emergencies and minor injuries.


Additionally, we offer funeral vehicles, known as sabbhan, for the respectful transportation of the deceased to the funeral service, cemetery, or crematorium. These vehicles are thoughtfully designed to securely carry the casket, leading the funeral procession from the funeral home to the final resting place.


For any assistance, please don't hesitate to contact our front desk or staff members at 9801071211. Your comfort and peace of mind are our utmost concern.
